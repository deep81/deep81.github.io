Intake 180 (One Hundred Eighty Only) As per the norms of Chaudhary Charan Singh University, Meerut as and when applicable with respect to change.

Prescribed application form can be obtained from the campus of Law College, Shikarpur, Bulandshahr (U.P.) or by sending a Bank Demand Draft of Rs. 500/- drawn in favour of the Saraswati Vidya Mandir Law College, payable at Shikarpur (Bulandshahr). The application form and registration are available in first 3 months of the session. Admission form can be obtained from website and be submitted to college with requisite fee of Rs/- 500.
