Admission Process

  • Procedure emphasized by C.C.S. University, Meerut. Merit after online registration.
  • It is compulsory for all students to mention all information truly and to affix latest photograph.
  • All students must submit transfer certificate, declaration and required fee within 5 days of The declaration of the merit list of admission.
  • Those candidate who comes from other universities than CCS University, Meerut must submit transfer certificate & character certificate and migration certification within 15days of admission.
  • Every document attached with admission form must base self attested.
  • The college reserves the right to cancel the admission at any time if any information provided by the student is found to be incorrect & misleading.
  • The student must gives declaration that he/she has not been found using unfair means in the University examination and never been convicted in any case under I.P.C.
  • The student shall also give under taking that he/she will not be engaged in any type of Ragging and unlawful activities is the college campus as well as outside the campus otherwise he/she will be personally liable for penalty or punishment including FIR and cancellation of admission from the college.
