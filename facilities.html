Facilities

  • Library
  • Moot Court
  • Computer Lab
  • Free legal Aid Clinic

We have a very rich library having latest Text books, Journals, Magazine, News Papers. The library is being managed by a qualified librarian. There are approximately 5000 books, law reports, and Periodicals, national and International Journals.

We have well furnished moot court room for the mooting activities in which the college provides practical knowledge about, how to contest and argue the case. Weekly mooting is compulsory.

The college has a computer lab in which internet facility is available. The student can search and understand the latest Supreme Court & High Court Cases and other legal activities through this lab.

We organize the legal aid camp and legal literacy programme through legal aid cell for the better achievement of their career. Free legal advice is also provided through leading practicing advocates and jurists.
