About

Saraswati Vidya Mandir Law College, Shikarpur, District-Bulandshahr has the unique distinction of being the first Law College (Self Finance) for LL.B.(3years) course. The permanent affiliation has been granted to the Chaudhary Charan Singh University, Meerut and recognized by Bar Council of India also. This Law College has been established on Meerut-Badaun National Highway 20 km away from Bulandshahr and 40 km away from Atomic Power Project, Narora.

This college is established by a registered society Satyanand Shiksha Samiti, Shikarpur. Shri Vipin Bansal, Manager/Secretary of the law college is a social worker and a man of extra ordinary vision. This vision has been translated in to practical reality through the establishment of education institution. His vision for the law college is to provide excellence in legal education & to produce quality lawyers, having moral principles & great human values.

Legal Education is Essential for entering in the legal profession and aspiring to become judge, advocate or legal consultants. Saraswati Vidya Mandir Law College, Shikarpur, Distt - Bulandshahr (U.P.) is known for excellence in legal education nurtured through perseverance and dedication of the faculty has been imparting quality legal education since 2005.

The college is running fulltime LL.B. (3year) and B.A.LL.B. (5year) Course. The courses are multi disciplinary in contents and equip the candidates with integrated knowledge of law. These courses are approved by bar council of India and affiliated to C.C.S. University, Meerut and designed to grasp the knowledge of all the developing field of Law such as Cyber Law, Intellectual Property Law, Banking & Insurance Law, Corporate Law and socio-economic Legislation in comprehensive manner with twin objectives to serve the public and individual as well.

Regular students and faculty interaction with leader from the legal & corporate world is helping us in grooming our students in to future luminance. Based on hard work and dedication, we dream to become the top most college with global vision in the days to come.
