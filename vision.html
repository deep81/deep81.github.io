Vision Statement

Mission

  • The College has its primary mission the creation of a generation of potential, performing and human legal experts.
  • To promote advance studies and research in all branches of law.
  • To evolve comprehensive legal education and to achieve excellence.
  • To promote cultural, legal and ethical values with a view to faster the rue of law and the objectives under the constitution of India.
  • To grace quality education with necessary practical training.
  • To prepare student for a variety of legal and law related career.

Vision

Saraswati Vidya Mandir Law College emphasizes the growth, development and formation of generation of its students with moral conscious. It aim is not for only good professionals but also good and worthy citizens of a great country aiding its overall progress and development. The college is for the making of judge, legislators and public servants or any way participants of judicial, parliamentary and executive system. It endeavours to treat every student as an individual to recognize their potential and to ensure they receive the best preparation and training for achieving their career ambitions and life goal.
